SISeG

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from parts of a pig's head, usually including the ears, cheeks, and jowls, along with chicken liver. These are boiled, chopped, and grilled, then seasoned with calamansi (a type of citrus), onions, chili peppers, and other spices. It's a popular appetizer or pulutan (a dish served with drinks) and is often served sizzling on a hot plate.

Ingredients:

  • pork
  • chili
  • calamansi
  • mayo
  • Knorr Liquid Seasoning: (Optional)
  • Oyster Sauce: (Optional)
  • Ginger: (Optional)
  • Eggs: (Optional, for topping)
  • Chicharon: (Optional, for topping and crunch)
  • Butter: (Optional, for sizzling)
  • Cooking Oil: For frying.
  • Garlic: Minced.
  • Soy Sauce or Fish Sauce: For seasoning.
  • Chili Peppers: Often sliced green chilies or chili flakes.
  • Onions: Regular onions and sometimes green onions or scallions.
  • Chicken Liver: (Optional, but common)

Procedure:

  1. Prepare the pork

    • Boil the pork belly and/or pig's head parts in water with salt, pepper, and sometimes ginger and bay leaves, until tender (about 40-60 minutes).
    • Grill or broil the boiled pork until slightly charred and crispy.
    • Chop the cooked pork into small, bite-sized pieces.

  2. Sauté Aromatics:

    • Heat cooking oil in a pan.
    • Sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
    • Add onions and cook until softened.
    • (Optional) Add chicken liver and cook until browned and mashed.

  3. Combine and Season:

    • Add the chopped pork to the pan with the sautéed aromatics.
    • Add soy sauce, fish sauce, calamansi or lemon juice, and chili peppers.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and other desired seasonings.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and other desired seasonings.
    • (Optional) Stir in crushed chicharon.

  4. Serve:

    • Heat a sizzling plate or cast iron pan.
    • Melt butter (optional) on the hot plate.
    • Transfer the sisig mixture to the hot plate.
    • (Optional) Crack an egg on top and stir until cooked.
    • Garnish with chopped green onions and chili peppers.
    • Serve immediately with rice.
